About RizalianMedHealth

Providing accessible and efficient healthcare services to the JRU community

Who We Are

RizalianMedHealth is JRU's innovative clinic reservation system designed to streamline healthcare access for students. Our platform bridges the gap between students and healthcare services, making medical assistance more accessible than ever.

Our Purpose

We aim to simplify the process of accessing medical services within the university, reducing wait times and improving the overall healthcare experience for students.

Key Features

  • Easy online appointment booking
  • Multiple healthcare services
  • Real-time availability updates
  • Secure student information handling
